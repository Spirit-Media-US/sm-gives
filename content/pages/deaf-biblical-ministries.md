# Deaf Biblical Ministries in Nagaland India

A school providing education, healthcare, housing, and spiritual guidance exclusively to deaf and poor students. Monthly sponsorship costs $25 per student.

## These are the 13 children that we have sponsored:

1. **Rahim and Rejikul** — Muslim brothers; father deceased (suicide), mother remarried; raised by grandmother
2. **Shingmei** — Phom tribe member from remote village
3. **Anjana** — Hindu girl from Nepal; mother blind, father disabled
4. **Tsaying** — 5-year-old from Sangtam tribe; parents are laborers
5. **Berila** — Yimkhung tribe member; parents are cultivators
6. **Sukuraj** — 10-year-old from Assam; father is daily wage laborer
7. **Ekhoudiheing** — From Manipur; parents cannot afford education
8. **Jangminlun** — From Peren District; parents are laborers
9. **Kana** — 7-year-old deaf girl; abandoned by father
10. **Phyobeni** — Multiple deaf family members; parents illiterate
11. **Nang Kana** — 12-year-old from Buddhist family in Assam
12. **Rhythm** — 10-year-old girl; deaf with epilepsy
