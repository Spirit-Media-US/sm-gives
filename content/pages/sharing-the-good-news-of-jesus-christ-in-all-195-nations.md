### January 30, 2024

# Sharing the Good News of Jesus Christ in All 195 Nations
 

The Great Commission, given by Jesus himself in [Matthew 28:19-20](https://www.bible.com/bible/111/MAT.28.19-20.NIV), is a powerful call to action for every believer: “Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you.” Sharing the Good News of Jesus Christ with every single person on Earth is an opportunity to experience God’s faithfulness and witness the power of the Gospel in action.

## The Landscape of Global Evangelism

Today, we live in a world with 195 recognized nations, each with its unique culture, language, and religious landscape. While the task of reaching every nation may seem monumental, it’s important to acknowledge the significant progress that has already been made. Through the tireless efforts of missionaries, evangelists, and everyday Christians, the Gospel has taken root in every corner of the globe.

- The Bible has been translated into over 3,300 languages, making it accessible to more people than ever before.

- Countless churches and ministries are actively engaged in evangelism, outreach, and discipleship across the world.

- Technological advancements like the internet and social media have opened up new avenues for sharing the Gospel and connecting with believers across borders.

## Challenges and Opportunities

Despite the progress, there are still challenges to overcome. Unreached people groups, cultural barriers, persecution, and political instability are just some of the obstacles that stand in the way of sharing the Good News. However, these challenges should not deter us. Instead, they should serve as a motivator to work even harder and find creative ways to reach those who haven’t yet heard about Jesus.

### Here are some ways we can all be involved in sharing the Good News in all 195 nations:

- **Pray**: Intercede for missionaries, and unreached people groups, and for God’s wisdom and guidance in evangelistic efforts.

- **Be active**: Consider participating in short-term mission trips or even long-term missionary service.

- **Share your faith**: Don’t be afraid to talk about your beliefs with friends, family, and neighbors. You never know who might be open to hearing the Good News.

- **Use technology**: Share your faith online through social media, blogs, and other digital platforms.

- **Give**: Financial support can help fund mission trips, Bible translations, and other vital ministries.
